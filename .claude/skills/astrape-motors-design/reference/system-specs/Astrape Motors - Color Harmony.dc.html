ASTRAPE MOTORS · COLOR HARMONY · TRIAD + COMPLEMENT
Every warm tone, derived from the blue.

The warm palette isn't picked by eye — it's rotated off the electric blue on the wheel. The triad (±120°) gives muted olive & plum that harmonize; the complement (180°) gives clay, the highest-tension hue, reserved for action. That shared geometry is why cool and warm sit together instead of clashing.
