01 — WHAT WE DO

We're a systems engineering firm.

Our specialty is making things move — with electric motors and software. At the core it's cybernetics: sense, compute, actuate — and close the loop. Systems engineering is the practice that holds it together — requirements, architecture, integration and V&V — so a platform behaves like one machine, not four subsystems that don't talk.

Actuate

Electric motors and power electronics — making it move.

WHAT WE DO MOST

Systems Integration

Whatever else a project gets called — electrification, controls, a test rig — underneath it's mostly this: taking subsystems that were never meant to work together — motors, inverters, controllers, sensors, software — and making them behave like one machine. Requirements, interfaces, architecture, bring-up and V&V. It's the work we do more than any other.
